Data Visual and Communicate

COURSE DESCRIPTION

In this course, students will be taught how to effectively communicate the results of the business analytics that they perform, in both written and oral presentation. The key questions for the analytic communications are: What is happening? Why is it happening? What should we do from here? The objective of this course is to enable students to learn:

  • What Data Visualization is
  • Understanding Business Insights
  • Best Methods of Communicating with Data
  • Table and Graph Design Best Practices
  • How to do Data Blending (Alteryx)
  • How to use a Data Visualization Tool (Tableau)
  • How to tell a Story

How is it useful?

Data visualizations make big and small data easier for the human brain to understand, and visualization also makes it easier to detect patterns, trends, and outliers in groups of data
